Who We Are


Alex Birkett

Alex Birkett is a digital marketing strategist and writer. He attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison, graduating with a B.A. degree in Journalism/Strategic Communications. Alex started his career working with the Grammy-nominated Shiny Toy Guns on their reformation, album launch, and North American tour marketing. He remains heavily involved in the music industry, offering marketing strategy to WUD Music, prolific regional musicians, and innovative producers. He also writes for Emmie Magazine and wudmusic.com. Alex has also worked for active lifestyles companies like Chestnut Mountain Resort and Arctica Race, capitalizing on his lifelong love of skiing, snowboarding, and outdoor sports in general. In his free time, Alex devours books, writes music, eats sushi, and drinks too much coffee. He writes at iamalexbirkett.com, on ThoughtCatalog, and for The Roosevelts.


Zeph Van Berlo

Zeph Van Berlo is a highly sought after media strategist and graphic designer from The Netherlands. After studying Communication & Information Studies in Utrecht, he moved overseas to Madison, WI, where he founded Orange Crown Media, working with renowned artists and businesses. Over the years, Zeph has worked with a variety of clients (local businesses, DJs/musicians, government agencies, and well-known brands) in multiple different countries. At the same time, Alex was running a successful freelance marketing business. After meeting for a few rounds of craft beers, Zeph and Alex decided to combine skills and work together to build something beautiful; thus, Nova Stella was born. Zeph, in addition to strategic management, executes search engine marketing campaigns and produces all graphic design at Nova Stella. In his free time, Zeph appreciates a good brew and likes to travel the world.
